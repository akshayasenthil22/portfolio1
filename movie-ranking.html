The Best Movies According to Akshaya

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

It takes viewers on a mesmerizing journey of self-discovery and romance as it follows the adventures of a spirited young group of friends.

Jaane Tu Ya Jaane Na

A delightful coming-of-age film that explores the complexities of friendship and love among a group of college friends.

Yeh Dil Hai Mushkil

This intricately weaves together love, heartbreak, and destiny in a tale that navigates the complexities of relationships with soul-stirring emotion.
